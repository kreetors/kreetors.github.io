About Kreetors

Kreetors was established in 2026 by five friends from Berkhamsted who shared a passion for creating fun and imaginative collectable trading cards.

The first Kreetors cards were designed using paper, pens and pencils. As the collection grew, the cards began to be professionally printed to create colourful, high-quality collectables that would last.

Some of the very first Kreetors included Worm, Spider and Chomper. These original characters helped shape the growing Kreetors universe.

Our goal is simple: to create fun, original and high-quality trading cards that children and collectors around the world can enjoy collecting, trading and sharing.

How Kreetors Started

Every Kreetors character begins as an original idea before being illustrated and turned into a collectable card. The collection continues to grow as new characters are created and added to the Kreetors world.
