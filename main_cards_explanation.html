Main Kreetors Cards

Meet some of the original Kreetors characters. Every Kreetor has its own appearance, strengths and abilities, making each trading card unique.

Worm

Worm is one of the most common Kreetors cards. Although it isn't one of the strongest fighters, it is quick and can escape danger more easily than many other Kreetors. Worm is a great card for new collectors and an important member of the Kreetors collection.

Pugerpillar

Pugerpillar was the very first Kreetors character to be created and remains one of the strongest. Despite its chunky appearance, it has excellent defensive abilities and can use its powerful back legs to kick opponents away when threatened.

Spidor

Spidor is a dangerous Kreetor thanks to its powerful venom. If enough venom reaches an opponent, it can be one of the most effective attackers. However, Spidor must get close to its target, making it vulnerable to Kreetors with long-range attacks.
